chicken torilla

Chicken tortilla is a delicious and hearty dish that combines tender pieces of seasoned chicken with crispy tortilla strips, all brought together in a rich, flavorful broth or layered as a casserole. Often made with ingredients like tomatoes, corn, black beans, and a blend of spices such as cumin and chili powder, it offers a satisfying mix of textures and bold, zesty flavors. Topped with cheese, avocado, sour cream, or fresh cilantro, chicken tortilla can be served as a comforting soup or baked meal, making it a versatile favorite in many kitchens.
